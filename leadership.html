Leadership

Lawrence McNutt, CEO and Co-Founder

With over thirty years of entrepreneurial expertise in founding and managing successful ventures, including Team-SOS, co-founded by Mr. McNutt in 1992. With his visionary leadership, he has led the company into the new world of telephony in 1995, the inception of computers and telephone integration. TeamSOS pioneered the world of VoIP, Unified Communications, and the Omni-Channel Contact Center, establishing themselves as multi-platform telephony experts at the beginning of this paradigm-shifting communications trend.

Mr. McNutt is passionate about giving to the community, and specifically about youth mentoring. The company has actively supported Big Brothers Big Sisters of Greater Sacramento for over 13 years. In addition to annual fundraising through their events and company participation in "Bowl for Kids Sake", Mr. McNutt has created a unique way to support this great charity through Speedway Motorcycle racing. Team-SOS has raced in local and national speedway venues since 2013, and has sponsored over 100 “Bigs” and “Littles” coming to the races.

Jeff Wilson, Vice President: Finance

Having over 35 years of experience in accounting, finance and taxation in technology companies, CPA firms and as an entrepreneur CPA. Jeff brought this experience and knowledge to Team-SOS joining the leadership team in September of 2019 to maximize efficiency and excellence in operational accounting and finance.
